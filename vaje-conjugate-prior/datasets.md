We list some example applications and datasets that can be analyzed with the beta-binomial model:
- political candidate support poll
- machine error rate in industrial applications
- psychometric questionnaire (https://www.bayesrulesbook.com/chapter-3.html)
- vaccine efficiency
- hospital treatment success rate (https://bayesball.github.io/BOOK/bayesian-hierarchical-modeling.html#example-deaths-after-heart-attack)
- loan approval (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/taweilo/loan-approval-classification-data)
- customer satisfaction (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/teejmahal20/airline-passenger-satisfaction)
- email spam filter efficiency (https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/balaka18/email-spam-classification-dataset-csv)
